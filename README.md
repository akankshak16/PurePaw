Description:
PurePaws is an online platform that offers health-conscious and eco-minded pet owners a
curated selection of premium organic pet food.
Goal and Mission:
Our goal is to deliver a seamless, transparent shopping experience that promotes pet health
and aligns with sustainability values. Our mission is to offer pet owners high-quality, organic pet
food that supports their pets' health and happiness. We are dedicated to transparency, eco-
friendly practices, and exceptional customer experiences, aiming to improve pet wellness and
care for the planet.
Target Audience:
The primary audience for "PurePaws" comprises pet owners prioritizing high-quality, organic pet
products. These users are likely to be environmentally aware, value ingredient transparency,
and seek products that reflect their commitment to sustainable living. Our target demographic
includes pet parents aged 25-55 with mid-to-high income levels, who are interested in holistic
pet care and eco-friendly practices.
